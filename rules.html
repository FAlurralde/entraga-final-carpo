8.2.      CONCEPT AND TYPES OF COOKIES

 “Cookies” are small text files stored on the User’s computer (or on another device, such as a laptop, tablet or smartphone) when he/she visits websites. They are very useful for ensuring that these sites function more efficiently: for example, if a User selects his/her preferred language on the “homepage”, the website will remember this preference and the User may continue browsing the website in his/her selected language.

FANDROID uses third party services to find out how you use this website and to optimise your User experience, amongst other things. These third parties may also use cookies, over which we have no control. In addition, you must bear in mind that the platforms for non-professional games and/or competitions that you access via the Website have their own cookies policies, which are not connected to FANDROID’s policy.

According to the function and the purpose of the cookies, they are normally divided into the following categories:

  1. Cookies that are strictly necessary: they allow you to move around the website and use its basic functions. These cookies are indispensable for using this website.
  2. Functionality cookies: these are used to recognise you when you access our website again and allow us to offer you more personalised functions, such as using your identifier, nickname or alias to greet you, and remembering your preferences. These cookies collect anonymous information and cannot track your movements on other websites.
  3. Analytics and performance cookies: these allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors, and also to gather information on how the website is used (e.g. which pages a visitor opens most frequently and whether the User receives error messages on some pages). This helps us to improve the way our website works, for example ensuring that Users find what they are looking for easily.
  4. Marketing and preferences cookies: these record your visit to our website, the pages you have visited and the links you have followed. We will use this information to show you advertisements that are more important for you and your interests, provided you have agreed for us to do so. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and to help to improve the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. We may also share this information with third parties (such as advertisers) for these purposes.

Depending on who owns them, cookies can be divided into two types: “first-party cookies” and “third-party cookies”:

  • “first-party cookies” are created by FANDROID and used by FANDROID to operate the Website.
  • “third-party cookies” are created by third parties not connected with FANDROID, which do not operate the Website and, consequently, are third-party companies not associated with FANDROID.
